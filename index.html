2602.12282 Broad absorptions in optical spectra of low-mass X-ray binaries

Abstract: We present the first population study of broad absorptions (BAs) in optical spectra of low-mass X-ray binaries. These features are ubiquitous in black hole systems (>50%), predominantly occurring in the hydrogen Balmer series across all X-ray states.
